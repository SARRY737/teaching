Time alloted for each task:

  1. Preparation time: 2 mins
  2. Cooking time : 5 mins
  3. Serving time : 1/2 mins

Ingredients:

  1. 1 Packet of maggie
  2. 1 Chopped onion
  3. 1 Chopped tomato
  4. 2 Chopped bell pepper
  5. 2 Tablespoon oil

Directions:

  • Add oil in a fry pan
  • Cook onion in high flame
  • Add tomato and masala mix, cook for a few more minutes
  • Add bell peppers
  • Add water and maggie and boil it for around 2-3 minutes
  • Your maggie is ready.
